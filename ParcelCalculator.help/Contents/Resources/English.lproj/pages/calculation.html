Calculation

It's very easy to get the combined dimensions of a parcel. Just enter height, width and length and Parcel Calculator will automatically calculate the values. The girth is used by a lot of shipping companys to determine price and conformity of a shipment. Additional calculated value is the volumetric weight, relevant for air freight. Volumetric weight is calculated on basis of the TACT rules, operated by IATA (International Air Transport Association). It comes into play whenever a parcel is treated as air freight.
