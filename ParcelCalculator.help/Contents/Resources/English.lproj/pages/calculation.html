Calculation

It is simple to get the girth of a parcel. Just enter height, width and length, Parcel Calculator will automatically calculate the values. The girth is used by a lot of delivery companies to determine price and conformity of a shipment. Second calculated value is the volumetric weight, sometimes referred as dimensional weight. It is based on TACT rules, operated by the IATA (International Air Transport Association) and comes into play whenever a parcel is treated as air freight. Parcel Calculator supports calculation with different measurement systems.
