Parcel Calculator uses Sparkle for automated updates. Hit the 'Update' button in the toolbar or the corresponding entry in the main menu to check for available updates. Call the settings panel to change general update behaviors. You can set up Parcel Calculator to search automatically for updates and you can even specify an interval for it (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly).
